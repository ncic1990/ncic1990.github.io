陈  欢

　　Jin Xiong (熊劲) received her B.S. degree in computer science from SiChuan University in 1990, and M.S. degree in computer architecture from Institute of Computing Technology, the Chinese Academy in 1993. She worked for a series of important projects of the National High Technology Development 863 Program of China, including Dawning 1000, 2000, 2000A, 3000, 3000L, 4000L and 4000A. She is currently an associate professor of Institute of Computing Technology, the Chinese Academy of Sciences. Meanwhile she is a Ph.D. candidate now and an IEEE member. Her research interests include operating systems, file systems, I/O in high-performance computers and networked storage systems.
