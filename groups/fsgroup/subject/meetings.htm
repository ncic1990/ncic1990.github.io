>> 高性能计算

  • SC: The International Conference for High Performance Computing and Communication , 每年11月举行（始于1989年）， Conference on High Performance Networking and Computing , http://www.sc-conference.org/

  • CLUSTER 2004, IEEE Int’l Conf. on Cluster Computing, San Diego, California, http://grail.sdsc.edu/cluster2004/

  • CCGRID 2004, 4th IEEE/ACM Int’l Symp. on Cluster Computing & the Grid, Chicago. http://www.mcs.anl.gov/ccgrid2004

  • HPCA-9：Ninth International Symposium on High Performance Computer Architecture,每年举办一次, http://www.cs.arizona.edu/hpca9

  • HPCS2004, The 18th International Symposium on High Performance Computing Systems and Applications, Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada,每年举办一次 , http://www.cs.umanitoba.ca/~hpcs04/

  • HPDC-13, 13th IEEE Int’l Symp. on High-Performance Distributed Computing, Honolulu. http://hpdc13.cs.ucsb.edu

>> 并行与分布式计算

  • IPDPS2004：International Parallel and Distributed Processing Symposium，Denver, Colorado,USA, http://www.ipdps.org/ipdps2004, 每年举办一次。

  • ICDCS04: The 24th International Conference on Distributed Computing Systems, Tokyo, Japan, http://www.cse.ohio-state.edu/icdcs04 ,由IEEE主办，开始于1979年，从84年起每年举办一次。这是分布式计算系统领域中历史最悠久的会议。

  • WORLDS’04: First Workshop on Real, Large Distributed Systems, December 5, 2004, San Francisco, California, USA, http://www.usenix.org/events/worlds04.

  • ICPADS 2004, 10th Int’l Conf. on Parallel & Distributed Systems, http://www.cacs.louisiana.edu/icpads2004/

  • PDCS2004: 17th International Conference on Parallel and Distributed Computing systems, San Francisco, CA USA, 每年举办一次,http://multimedia.ece.uic.edu/pdcs2004/

  • FTDCS2004：International Workshop on Future Trends in Distributed Computing Systems，Suzhou, China, 每一到两年举办一次, http://www.cercs.gatech.edu/ftdcs2004

>> Operating Systems

  •  SOSP: ACM Symposium on Operating Systems Principles ，每两年举行（始于1967年），上一次是2003年http://www.cs.rochester.edu/sosp2003/ 

  • USENIX04: USENIX Annual Technical Conference, http://www.usenix.org/events/usenix04/ 

  • OSDI04: Sixth Symposium on Operating Systems Design and Implementation, 每两年一次（始于1994年，已举办过的有OSID94, OSID96, OSID99, OSID00, OSID02, OSID04），http://www.usenix.org/events/osdi04/ 

  • HotOS: Workshop on Hot Topics in Operating Systems, 每两年举办一次，上一次是第九次，在Lihue, Hawaii, USA, May 18-21, 2003, http://www.usenix.org/events/hotos03/

  • 2004 OASIS Workshop: The 1st Workshop on Operating System and Architecture Support for the on demand IT Infrastructure. Oct9, 2004, Boston, Massachusetts. http://www.research.ibm.com/actc/OASIS-2004 .

  • ASPLOS XI: 11th International Conference on Architecture Support for Programming Languages and Operating Systems, Boston Massachusetts, http://www.eecg.toronto.edu/asplos2004. 从1982年开始，1987年后基本上每两年举办一次。

  • BSDCON03: The premier conference for the BSD community , 只举行过两次（2002，2003），http://www.usenix.org/publications/library/proceedings/bsdcon03/
