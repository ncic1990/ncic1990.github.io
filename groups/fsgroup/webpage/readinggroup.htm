HPCFS group has held its weekly seminars since this year(2005), where we discuss, and criticize classical and recently published papers , unpublished reports, research ideas, and results including many of our on-going projects. The seminars sometimes combine with the group's colloquium talks. Here is a list of the contents of the past and coming seminars

In usual time,the seminars are held in north buliding 252 at 2:30pm on Thursday, everyone else in our institute will be welcomed.
