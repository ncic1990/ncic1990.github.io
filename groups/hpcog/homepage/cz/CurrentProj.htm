Raindrop System , name of our optical interconnect evaluation system,
is one of the fundamental parts of DSAG. Raindrop system uses the DDR interface as
the communication channel with the computer system, and uses FPGA to implement
DDR control logic. RocketIO Multi-Gbit transceivers inside Xilinx FPGA are used for
high speed transmit and receive logic. 12-way VCSEL electronic-optical and PIN
optical-electronic modules are used for optical interconnect. With DDR266 and 12
RocketIO transceivers, the total bandwidth can reach 25Gb.
