 事务处理服务器的性能评价研究

事务处理服务器的性能评价研究

在中小型商业应用的数据库性能评测中，现有的数据库基准存在着度量结果缺乏实际应用意义、测试成本过高和不能检测系统瓶颈等问题。本文提出了一种建立特定领域数据库基准的方法，它可以用来解决建立数据库基准必须面对的负载事务选取、描述和基准实现中系统性能优化等技术问题。本文并以金融证券业为例，详细论述了这种方法的应用。
本文主要完成了下面的工作：
本文提出了一种可用于中小型商业数据库系统性能评价的基准，全面定义了它的数据库结构，事务和性能度量，企业可以方便地用来评价现有的服务器和数据库系统，便于了设备的选型和改进。
针对目前基准负载设计中以经验为主、缺乏量化方法的问题，本文对基准负载设计中涉及的概念给出了量化的定义，更准确地表示了基准负载需求，并给出了评价基准负载代表性和简单性的尺度，提供了几种可利用的基准负载生成算法。
根据软件工程的思想，结合基准负载需求，本文提出了一种切实可行的基准负载生成方法。在方法中结合了用户行为图和操作对象归类等技术，而且利用这种方法成功地得出了SDB（证券业数据库基准）负载。
为了克服自然语言的歧义性和预防在系统测试中的基准禁止，本文定义了一种形式化事务描述语言—TSL，并利用它给出了SDB 基准负载事务的准确定义和ACID 特性的具体描述，这种形式化定义为基准测试程序的自动生成和执行提供了可能。
系统优化对性能测试数值有重要影响。针对目前系统优化困难的现状，本文提出了一种适合SDB 基准测试和中小型商业应用的从上而下的优化方法，并对此方法中涉及的参数给出了建议数值和评判标准。
曙光3000 超级服务器是国产超级服务器的代表。我们在曙光3000 上实现了SDB 基准程序，并给出了它一个服务结点的SDB 性能数值，说明了一个服务结点可以满足现有中小型商业应用，为曙光3000 超级服务器进入国内金融证券等商业领域提供了性能数值上的依据。
本文还利用SDB 基准分析了目前证券业数据库系统，找出了它们普遍存在的问题，提出了改进的建议。最后，本文借助排队论和层次分析方法给出了SDB中性能度量数值内在的实际意义。
本文提出的特定领域基准生成方法和SDB将用于苏州实验室性能评价研究。
关键词：数据库基准，负载，性能优化，证券交易，ACID，性能测量和评价
事务处理服务器的性能评价研究

        Performance Evaluation Research of Transaction Server
                  Changyong Gu(Computer Science)
                    Directed by Prof. Zhiwei Xu

In the field of performance evaluation of database system in the small or medium-sized enterprises, existing database benchmarks have many problems, such as meaningless metric results in practical applications, costly test implement. They also cannot discover the system bottlenecks. We present a effective way to create the domain-specific benchmarks. By this means, we solve some key technical difficulties in creating database benchmark: taking out benchmark transaction workload, describing transaction and optimizing system performance in the process of implement. We take a example of financial securities to discuss how to utilize this method in details.

The dissertation mainly completes the following tasks:
The paper introduces a benchmark, which is usable to measure database
system performance of small or medium-sized enterprise. We define its
data structure, transaction and performance metric in details. The SDB
successfully solve the problems of performance evaluation in securities
and other trades, facilitating to choose and improve system.

Current benchmark workloads mainly generate from experience, which lacks of quantitative instruction. The dissertation quantitatively defines concepts in workload development, more accurately illustrates
requirement of workload and gives the evaluation standard of workload.
The paper also describes some good arithmetic to produce benchmark
workload.

According to theory of software engineering, combining with characteristics of workload requirement of database benchmark, the paper presents a practicable way to create workload. In this way, we integrate user behavior graphs and object-operated classifications to generate highly representative workload. In terms of this method, we get triumphantly a Securities Database Benchmark (SDB) workload.

Due to amphibology of natural language, the paper defines a formal
Transaction Specification Language (TSL) in order to prevent the benchmark specials. I utilize it to define more accurately workload
specification and ACID property of SDB. Moreover, this formal method
makes automatic generation and execution of SDB programs possible.

System optimization has a immediate effect on the data of performance
metrics. In order to solve the difficulties in current system tuning,
the paper presents a from-up-to-down optimization method, which is
suitable to SDB tests and applications of small or medium-sized
enterprises. The paper also shows the reference data of the parameters
and standards of the judgement.

Dawning3000 super server is a representative of domestic super server
series. We implement the SDB program on this platform and look into
performance of one of its nodes, and prove that one node fulfills
requirements of the medium-sized or small enterprise, which gives actual support to apply these server nodes in these trade fields, such as stocks.

Based on SDB, the paper analyses the securities database system. We
find out the general problem in this trade and give the suggestions how
to improve. In the end of the paper, by the support of queue theories
and hiberarchy analyses, the internal meanings of the SDB metrics are
illustrasted.

The method to create the domain-specific benchmark and SDB are given
to Suzhou lab for studing performance evaluation.

Keywords: Database benchmark, Workload, Performance optimization,
Securities exchange, ACID, Performance Measurement and Evaluation
