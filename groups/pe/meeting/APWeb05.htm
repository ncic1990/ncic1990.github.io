  The Seventh Asia Pacific Web Conference  
 

APWeb'05 is the seventh in a series of international conferences on WWW Technologies.  APWeb?5 aims at bringing together researchers and practitioners from both academic and industry to exchange knowledge on WWW-related technologies and new advanced applications. Previous APWeb conferences were held in Beijing (1998), Hong Kong (1999), Xi'an (2000), Changsha (2001), Xi'an (2003) and Hangzhou (2004).
