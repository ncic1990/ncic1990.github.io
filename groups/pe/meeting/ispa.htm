Call For Papers

Third International Symposium on Parallel and Distributed Processing and Applications

(ISPA2005)

Following the traditions of previous successful ISPA conferences, ISPA '03  (held in Aizu-Wakamatsu City, Japan) and ISPA '04 (held in Hong Kong), the objective of ISPA '05 is to provide a forum for scientists and engineers in academia and industry to exchange and discuss their experiences, new ideas, research results, and applications about all aspects of parallel and distributed computing and networking. ISPA '05 will feature session presentations, workshops, tutorials and keynote speeches.

 

Topics of particular interest include, but are not limited to:

l Computer networks

l Network routing and communication algorithms

l Parallel/distributed system architectures

l Tools and environments for software development

l Parallel/distributed algorithms

l Distributed systems and applications

l Wireless networks, mobile and pervasive computing

l Reliability, fault-tolerance, and security

l Performance evaluation and measurements

l Grid and cluster computing 

l Internet computing and Web technologies

l Database applications and data mining

l High-performance scientific and engineering computing

 

Submission Procedure：Submissions should include an abstract, key words, the e-mail address of the corresponding author, and must not exceed 15 pages, including tables and figures, with PDF, PostScript, or MS Word format. Electronic submission through the submission website is strongly encouraged. Hard copies will be accepted only if electronic submission is not possible. Submission of a paper should be regarded as an undertaking that, should the paper be accepted, at least one of the authors will register and attend the conference to present the work.

Publication:The proceedings of the symposium will be published in Springer's Lecture Notes in Computer Science. A selection of the best papers for the conference will be published in a special issue of The Journal of Supercomputing and International Journal of High Performance Computing and Networking (IJHPCN).
