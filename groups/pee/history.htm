Completed Projects:

    May,2002-- May,2003, research and develop ServerScope server performance evaluation equipment. This is an Innovation Project in ICT. After thoroughly research in current kinds of performance evaluation system, combine Distributed Components Technology and Blade Computer Technology, orient to customer's continuously changing demand of evaluation but not manufacturer oriented, we put forward and realize a kind of new server performance evaluation system. This system is low cost, scalable and easy to use. The main research content includes a special cluster equipment constitutes with blade computers and server performance evaluation software.
　

    November,2003 -- February,2004， complete a SPECsfs performance evaluation of R220-A1 sever for the combined laboratory of Dawning and AMD company.
