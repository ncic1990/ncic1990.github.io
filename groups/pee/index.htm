Performance Evaluation Team
Projects History & Achievements Database Links Team Life

　

    The Performance Evaluation (PE) team in National Research Center for intelligent computing systems (NCIC) is established in August of 2001, belonging to the business calculation group in NCIC.

    The PE team concentrates on research and development of the server performance evaluation. It supports some large research and development project in NCIC, and also undertakes project in related field that match the guideline and strategy of Institute of Computing Technology (ICT), Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS).

    The PE team has eight members now. There are three Doctors graduate students, two Master's graduate student. Three formal employees are all Master's educational background.
