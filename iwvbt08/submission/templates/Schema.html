Schema of the database:
  • Paper (id, title, authors, emailContact, abstract, topic, status, isUploaded, format, fileSize, submission_date)
  • Author (id_paper, position, last_name, first_name, affiliation)
  • PCMember (email, firstName, lastName, affiliation, idAdmin)
  • Review (idPaper, email, overall, reviewerExpertise, summary, details, comments, isValid, submission_date, last_revision_date)
  • ResearchTopic (id, label)
  • SelectedTopic (email, idTopic)
  • ReviewMark (idPaper, email, criteria, mark)
  • RateLabel(id, label)
  • Rating (idPaper, email, rate, significance)
  • Criteria (id, label, weight)
  • PaperStatus (id, label, mailTemplate, cameraReadyRequired)
  • Message (idMessage, idParent, idPaper, emailReviewer, message, date)
