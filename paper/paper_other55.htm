 信息网格中访问控制机制的研究

 
信息网格中访问控制机制的研究

摘　　要

访问控制是信息网格中资源共享的关键问题之一。信息网格中的资源是广域分布的，处于不同组织的控制之下，并且资源本身可能是异构的。信息网格是一个由访问控制机制和安全策略不同的异构管理域组成的环境，不同的域可能有不同的访问控制目标和要求。信息共享要求能够跨越管理域的边界进行授权，共享关系处于受控状态，将那些资源共享给谁都应得到明确的定义；同时共享机制不能强制改变本地的访问控制机制，需要保持本地控制的自主性原则，即在各个管理域中，可以独立的指定本管理域的访问控制策略，保留对资源最终的控制权。网格强调技术上的通用开放标准和控制方式上的非集中性，任何与特定应用场景强相关的或以集中控制为主的跨域授权解决方案都不是所期望的。本文结合中科院计算技术研究所的ICTGrid企业信息网格项目，研究了社区内部基于角色访问控制的实现和满足跨社区共享需要的授权与验证机制。

论文首先介绍了网格技术的概念和相关背景以及织女星信息网格的设计思想和体系结构；其次通过对信息网格环境的分析，总结了访问控制的关键问题；然后提出了跨社区授权的解决方法两阶段授权与验证，阐述它的概念、方法和过程；随后给出了ICTGrid中访问控制的实现与分析；最后，对论文的工作进行了总结。

两阶段授权与验证机制是本文研究的主要内容，它的基本思想是首先将共享权限作为一个整体授予使用资源的社区,然后再由它对社区内的用户进行权限分配。通过将跨社区的授权和验证分为功能和目的不同的两个阶段来实现,可以较好的满足信息网格中资源共享对访问控制的需要。

关键词：信息网格，访问控制，两阶段授权与验证，共享机制



Study of Access Control Mechanism in Information Grid

Abstract

Access control is one of the key issues in the study of resource sharing in information grid. In information grid information resources are distributed in wide areas, controlled by different organizations and may be heterogeneous. In this paper, the heterogeneity of resource is not discussed and the topic is on the focus of the access control issue in sharing mechanism. The system of information grid consists of heterogeneous administrative domain in which the access control mechanism and security policy are not uniform. The purpose and need of access control are also different in various organizations. Information sharing requests authorization across the boundaries of organizations and the sharing relations are under control, that is to say, which resources are shared to someone should be defined definitely. At the same time sharing mechanism can not force local systems to change their security mechanism in substance .The principle of autonomous control requires that each domain should specify its access policy and keep in the final control of the resource. Grid technology put emphasis upon versatile and open standard and decentralized control. So, any across-domain authorization solution, which is concerned with special application scenario or is mainly by means of centralized control, is not expected. This paper studies the realization of RBAC in community and the mechanism of two-phase authorization and validation across communities combined with the ICTGrid research project in ICT of CAS.

First, this dissertation introduces the conceptions and background of grid technology and the design principle and architecture of VEGA information grid; then, this dissertation discusses the key issues of access control in information grid through analyzing the environment character of information grid; after that, it provides the concept, method and process of two-phase authorization and validation as a solution; then, it provides the realization and analysis of access control mechanism in ICTGrid ;at last, this dissertation summarizes the author’s research and discusses some related questions.

The two-phase authorization and validation mechanism is central in the paper. In two-phase authorization method, permissions of sharing resources are granted to the community using them as a whole at first and in the second phase end users are authorized in the control of the community. Two-phase authorization and validation method dividing the task into two phases by different function and purpose can meet the need of access control mechanism for resource sharing in information grid.

Key Words：Information Grid, Two-Phase Authorization and Validation, Access Control, Sharing Mechanism
