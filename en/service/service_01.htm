About the National High Performance Computing Center(NHPCC, Beijing)

　　The Dawning-1000 Massively Parallel Processing System was successfully developed in May, 1995. President Song Jian of the Chinese Academy of Engineering (the State Councilor and director of the Commission of Sciences) indicated that some national high performance computing centers would be established with the Dawning-1000 and a lot of work would be done for extending the application of the Dawning-1000, then NHPCC (Beijing) was founded. Seven years passed and nowadays NHPCC (Beijing) has Dawning-1000A, Dawning 2000-I and Dawning-3000, one set for each type, which has strong computing ability. Now Sun Ning-Hui is the Director of NHPCC and Feng Sheng-Zhong is the Deputy Director.
　　According to incomplete statistics, in recent seven years NHPCC has supported 450 great projects including National Natural Science Fund, Reconstruction Plan, State-funded research problem-solving projects, “863” High-Tech Projects and many important programs for other committees and all of these has obtained many very influential achievements. These applications cover numerous realms such as physics, chemistry, mechanics, petroleum, nuclear energy, meteorology, aeronautics, astronautics, hydraulics, biomedicine, bio-informatics, etc. NHPCC (Beijing) has accumulated much technology and has gained great reputation in study of system maintenance, technology support, performance evaluation, application and implementation, and parallel model and algorithm.

　　NHPCC (Beijing) has been undertaking “863” projects and National Natural Science Fund and at the other hand we cooperates with many international famous software corporations to develop the transplantation and testing for CAE software. Those Study interests include fundamental study of parallel computing, development of high performance parallel software and algorithm, development of high performance parallel library, technology service for high performance computing, parallelization of application software, and high performance application integration.

　　The high performance parallel system is a key high-tech in the world, which is very close to the development of national economy and enhancement of overall national power. The success of the Dawning server series founded a high substantial basis to develop high performance computing, but the lag of parallel software and parallel algorithm has become a main bottleneck to high performance parallel system.

　　NHPCC (Beijing) will make a great effort to find a way to convert research results into productivity and try our best to satisfy the requirement for the important large-scale computing in the national economy and national defense construction. Based on Dawning supercomputers, software development and application integration will be utilized to strengthen the study of algorithm design and methodology. At the same time research and development work will be combined with service into one system. We will take advantage of our new research results to do more contributions to the application of high performance computer for our country.
