 基于HFC的视频点播系统

 
基于HFC的视频点播系统


视频点播(VOD)业务是未来宽带网中最有前途的业务之一 。它可以让用户控制节目的播放---选择播放时间与播放的形式，如快进、快退、暂停等，它与传统电相比有着无可比拟的优点。在各种宽带网络上实现视频点播业务成为当前多媒体和网络领域的一个研究热点。

本论文详细讨论了在光纤同轴混合网(HFC)上实现视频点播系统的各种相关问题，概要描述了曙光VOD的实现方案，着重点在于基于HFC并满足MPEG－2/DVB标准的视频点播系统与普通视频点播系统的不同之处。

本论文除了介绍VOD的背景知识外，重点描述了作者在以下几个方面的工作：
1. 磁盘调度算法研究
2. 视频服务器中实现MPEG－2传输流的优化方案
3. 视频服务器软件设计
4. 满足实时要求的Windows NT驱动程序设计

【关键词】视频点播；光纤同轴混合网；MPEG－2；DVB；传输流；磁盘调度


Video On Demand will be one of the most successful services in the emerging broadband network. It offers customers a large video library to watch at any time they desire, and allows them to use VCR-style functions such as pause, rewind and fast-forward. It has distinguished advantages over existing TV system. To implement Video On Demand (VOD) on various broadband networks is currently a hotspot in multimedia and network research.

This thesis discusses various questions related to implementation of VOD on Hybrid Fiber Coaxial-cable (HFC) network, outlines the project of Dawn VOD System, emphasizes the difference between our VOD system satisfying MPEG－2/DVB and other VOD systems on ordinary network such as ATM.

Besides explaining background knowledge of VOD, this thesis mainly describes the work of the author in the following areas:
1. Disk scheduling algorithm.
2. How to optimally implement MPEG-2 transport stream in video server.
3. Design of video server software.
4. Design of Windows NT driver satisfying real-time requirement.

【 Key words 】Video On Demand(VOD)；Hybrid Fiber Coaxial-cable (HFC)；MPEG－2；DVB；Transport stream；Disk scheduli
