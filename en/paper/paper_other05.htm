 信息网格访问控制的研究

 
信息网格访问控制的研究


信息网格是利用网格技术实现信息资源的共享、管理和提供信息服务的系统。本文结合中科院计算所织女星信息网格计划的研究工作，研究了信息网格的访问控制机制。

本文提出了一个在信息网格的由用户(U)、信息空间(IS)和操作(OP)这三个正交的概念构成的三维(U, IS, OP)空间中研究信息网格访问控制的理论方法，这是研究信息网格访问控制的基础。(U, IS, OP)空间为形式化的研究信息网格的访问控制提供了一个方法，本文在这个空间中定义了信息网格访问控制的基本概念。

织女星信息网格的指导思想是通用服务、辅助智能、全局一体和自主控制。本文根据织女星信息网格的指导思想和信息网格的需求特点描述了信息网格访问控制研究的关键问题，并在(U, IS, OP)空间定义了其中的通用性等问题。信息网格访问控制研究的这几个关键问题是信息网格访问控制的特征。

本文分析了中科院计算所智能中心开发的企业信息平台的访问控制机制，这是信息网格研究的相关领域，已经在企业和科研部门中得到应用。论文由企业信息平台的实践进一步提出信息网格访问控制需要解决的问题。

本文在NIST的RBAC建议标准的基础上提出了IG-RBAC模型，IG-RBAC模型是在(U, IS, OP)空间中基于角色的信息网格访问控制模型。论文定义了IG-RBAC模型中角色和角色继承的概念，证明了IG-RBAC模型的通用性，并提出以角色统一用户和权限的概念。IG-RBAC模型是织女星信息网格研究的一个组成部分。

关键词：织女星信息网格，访问控制，角色，企业信息平台

Research on Access Control of the Information Grid
Zhang Gang (Computer Architecture)
Directed By Xu Zhiwei

Information Grid is a system using the Grid technologies to achieve the sharing, managing of information resources and to provide information services. This dissertation is based on the research work of the Vega Information Grid project, and addresses the access control mechanism of the information grid.

In this dissertation, a theoretic method of the research in the access control of the information grid in the (U, IS, OP) space which is consisted of the three orthogonal concepts of Users(U), Information Space(IS) and Operations(OP) of information grid were proposed, and this is the foundation of the research work in the access control of the information grid. The Space provides a method to research the access control of the information grid formalized, and we define the key concepts of the access control of the information grid in the space.

The principles of the Vega Information Grid is Versatile Service, Enable Intelligence, Global Uniformity and Autonomous Control. This dissertation describes the essential issues of the research work of the information grid based on the principles and main requirement features of the Vega Information Grid, and defines the problem of versatile in the (U, IS, OP) space. These essential issues are the characters of the access control of the information grid.

This dissertation analyzes the access control mechanism of the Enterprising Information Portal developed by the NCIC, ICT, which is a correlative research field of the information gird, and has been applied in enterprising and research institute. This dissertation presents more problems that should be resolved by the access control mechanism of the information grid based on the implement of the Enterprising Information Portal.

This dissertation presents the IG-RBAC model which is based on the proposed NIST standard for role-based access control, this model is the role-based access control model of information grid and it is defined in the (U, IS, OP) space. This dissertation defines the concepts such as role and the role hierarchy, proves the versatile of the IG-RBAC model, and presents the unification of concepts of roles, users and permissions. The IG-RBAC model is a part of the research work of the Vega Information Grid.

Keywords: Vega Information Grid, access control, role, Enterprising Information Portal
