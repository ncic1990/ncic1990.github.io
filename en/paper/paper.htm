From NCIC, there graduated hundreds of master and Ph.D graduates. While participating in the research projects, they fused their knowledge and research well, and wrote high-quality degree thesis. During the research, they also published excellent academic papers.

　　To support the users to work in the National High Performance Computing Center, the government founded National High performance Computing Foundation during the ninth five-year-plan period. The foundation has funded nearly 340 projects from 1995 to 2000. Up to November, 2001, 450 projects has been performed in the center. Most of the projects are sponsored by the National Natural Science Fund, Reconstruction Projects, National Research Problem-solving Projects, 863 Programs, 973 Programs and other key programs. Nearly 400 projects have been completed and obtained many influential achievements and high-quality academic papers.
