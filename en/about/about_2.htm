Center Structure
       
    Institute of Computing Technology of Chinese Academy of Sciences offers service of supporting in strong logistics for the National Research Center for Intelligent Computing Systems(NCIC). The center implements director's responsibility system and three-dimensional management system of administration, academics and projects. Administrative organization consists of the administrative staff (director, deputy director, assistant to director, business secretary and administrative secretary) and the research groups. The research groups are non-permanent organs, organized in academic direction, divided into several subgroups and implementing the responsibility system of differentiated control. The leaders of groups and subgroups are responsible for steering the academic direction of the relevant field; In-process project leader manages the projects (tasks). Group members may step over more than one group. The board of trustee made up of director, deputy director, group leader manages the relevant affairs of the Center.
