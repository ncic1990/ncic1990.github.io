Besides supports of the government and leaders at all levels, running a national-level high-tech research and development centre well two conditions must be possessed: First, there should be a correct technology route, second, there should be a group of high-tech talents with a common goal.

The intelligence and wisdom of China's first-class computer scientific research personnel may not be lower than that of foreigners. So long as attracting a group of fighters who do solid work, do not admire vanity and are determined to strive and innovate for revitalizing the national high-tech industry, we should be able to accomplish whatever foreign first-class computer laboratory can do.
