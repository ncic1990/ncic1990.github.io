Overview
       
　　At the beginning of June 2001, Dawning came into Hong Kong main boards successfully and became a wholly-owned subsidiaries of Shenzhen Tech company. This landmark will offer a wider operation stage of market for full series of Dawning products. Along 10 years, the Dawning company made a proud achievement, and the sales amount increases year by year, steadily, fast.
Reports of authoritative market-studying organization in 2001 show Dawning is one of the three leaders in domestic server market, the only leader in high-end server market and ranks the third overall. Dawning becomes one of the brands of server that has influence power most. The way of super server industrialization of the Dawning leaves wider and wider. Besides, because the Dawning competes with foreign big company in the high-end server market, foreign manufacturers have to adjust prices one after another. And for the reason of our country imports the mainframe computer at the level of about 100 sets every year, Dawning economizes the fund of several hundred million yuan for the country every year alone. The appearing on the market of super server of Dawning has broken the situation of dominating the Chinese supercomputer market exclusively of the foreign products and rewritten game rules of the high-performance computer market of our country. The products that the Dawning put out have already become an important reference for U.S.A. to make its export policy.
